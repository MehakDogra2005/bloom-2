About Bloom

At Bloom, we believe that understanding your menstrual cycle is key to unlocking better mental health and emotional well-being. Hormonal changes throughout your cycle significantly impact your mood, energy levels, and cognitive function. By tracking and understanding these patterns, you can work with your body rather than against it.

Our mission is to provide simple, mindful solutions that help you navigate each phase of your cycle with grace and self-compassion. Because when you understand your body, you can better care for your mind.

Understanding Your Cycle

Your menstrual cycle consists of four distinct phases, each with unique effects on your mental and emotional state.

Menstrual Phase

Days 1-5: Low energy, increased need for rest and self-care. This is a time for introspection and gentle activities.

Mental state: You may feel more intuitive but also more sensitive. Honor your need for quiet time.

Follicular Phase

Days 6-14: Rising energy levels, increased motivation and creativity. Ideal for starting new projects.

Mental state: You'll likely feel more optimistic and social. Take advantage of this productive window.

Ovulation Phase

Days 15-17: Peak energy, confidence, and social desire. Your body is primed for connection.

Mental state: Verbal skills and charisma are at their highest. Great time for important conversations.

Luteal Phase

Days 18-28: Energy declines, PMS symptoms may appear. Focus shifts inward as progesterone rises.

Mental state: You may feel more anxious or irritable. Practice self-compassion and stress management.
